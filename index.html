About Us

What We Do

We aim to empower women to achieve economic independence by providing a network of support, professional attire and the development tools to help women thrive in work and in life. Our vision is a world where women do not live in poverty; are treated with dignity and respect; and are strengthening their families and shaping their communities.

Our Story

In 1996, Nancy Lublin received an inheritance from her great-grandfather and turned it into one that would keep giving. She teamed up with three nuns and founded Dress for Success in the basement of a church in Manhattan. The mission spread, demonstrating that economic independence is a challenge that all women face.

Who We Are

Dress for Success is a global not-for-profit organization that empowers women to achieve economic independence by providing a network of support, professional attire, and tools to thrive in work and in life. Since 1997, we have expanded to almost 150 cities in 25 countries and helped more than 1.2 million women towards self-sufficiency.
